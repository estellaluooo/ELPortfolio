Case Study × UGREEN

Large-scale brand activations and experiential marketing campaigns

Project Overview

Brand: Ugreen

Event: 2025 NYC Marathon Expo

Duration: 3 days

Objective

Increase brand visibility among runners and fitness enthusiasts. Drive on-site engagement through interactive experiences. Generate UGC and social buzz during the Expo.

Strategy

O2O (Offline-to-Online) — Combined on-site Expo engagement with city-wide brand visibility to maximize reach beyond the event venue.

OOH Advertising — Deployed a branded bus circulating around Manhattan, featuring Ugreen's visual identity for repeated exposure across high-density urban routes.

Event + City Synergy — Leveraged the overlap between the Marathon Expo and Manhattan foot traffic to reinforce brand recall among runners, spectators, and NYC residents.

Outcome

Led experiential and influencer marketing activation at the New York Marathon Expo, engaging 80+ KOLs and supporting future Boston Marathon (2024) partnership development.
